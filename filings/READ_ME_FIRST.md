## ☑ THE ONE BIG CORRECTION FROM FINAL VERIFICATION — READ THIS

Earlier analyses (including in this repo) assumed your appeal belonged in **Circuit
Court** and the DCA routing was a mistake. **That was based on pre‑2021 law and is
WRONG.** Chapter 2020‑61, Laws of Florida, amended **§ 26.012, Fla. Stat.** effective
**January 1, 2021**: appeals from county court final judgments now go **directly to the
District Court of Appeal**. **The Second DCA is the CORRECT court for your appeal.**
The defect was in your Notice of Appeal saying "Circuit Court" — a fixable wording
error, not a jurisdiction problem. Documents 02 and 03 fix it.
